Abdelfattah Abouelaoualim is a seasoned expert in cloud architecture, data engineering, machine learning, and DevOps, boasting over 17 years of extensive experience across multiple industries. Holding a Ph.D. in Computer Science from Université Paris-Saclay, a Master's degree in Mathematics and Computer Science from ENSIMAG, and a Specialized Master's in Big Data from Télécom Paris, Abdelfattah combines deep academic knowledge with practical expertise.

Certified as a TOGAF Enterprise Architect and possessing multiple Google Cloud Platform certifications—including Professional Cloud Architect, Data Engineer, Machine Learning Engineer, and DevOps Engineer—he excels in designing sophisticated data platforms, managing high-performance data pipelines, and developing robust machine learning solutions.

Throughout his career, Abdelfattah has successfully led transformative projects for prominent organizations such as Vinci Airports and Bull SAS. His ability to merge cutting-edge technology with strategic insight has consistently enabled teams to achieve efficiency, innovation, and security at scale.

Abdelfattah is passionate about automation, cybersecurity, and advanced analytics, always staying ahead with continuous learning and innovation.
